Games and Interactive Tools

A small collection of interactive actuarial and risk-management tools. Some are serious. Some are intentionally silly.

Actuarial Notation Matching Game

Match actuarial symbols such as Aₓ, qₓ, pₓ, and äₓ with their definitions. A tiny game for students learning actuarial notation.

Weather Betting Game

A simplified weather derivative simulator. Guess the season's HDD outcome, choose a contract size, and see whether your weather bet pays off.
